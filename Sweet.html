Cupcake

Delicious caramel cupcake topped with smooth caramel frosting.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Caramel sauce for topping

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). In a bowl, cream together the butter and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Combine flour and baking powder, add to the creamed mixture and mix well. Finally, stir in the milk until the batter is smooth. Pour or spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Let cupcakes cool before frosting with caramel sauce.

Cheesecake

Creamy cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter, and press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Mix in the sour cream until smooth. Pour the filling into the crust. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is almost set. Let cool before refrigerating for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tiramisu

Classic Italian dessert layered with coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups brewed coffee, cooled
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions:

In a large bowl, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale. Add mascarpone cheese and mix until smooth. In another bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Gently fold whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture. Dip ladyfingers into the coffee and layer in a dish. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat with another layer of coffee-dipped ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Cookies

Chewy chocolate chip cookies with a crisp edge and soft center.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). In a bowl, combine flour and baking soda. In another large bowl, cream together butter, sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla. Gradually add the flour mixture and mix until combined. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool on wire racks.
