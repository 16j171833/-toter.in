Pizza

Delicious homemade pizza with a crispy crust and savory toppings.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, olives, etc.)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and add your desired toppings. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and serve hot.

Shrimp Noodles

A savory dish with succulent shrimp and flavorful noodles.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz noodles
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add garlic and shrimp, cooking until the shrimp turns pink. Remove shrimp and set aside. In the same skillet, add sesame oil and mixed vegetables, sautéing until tender. Return the shrimp to the skillet, add the cooked noodles and soy sauce, and toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper. Serve warm.

Grape Leaves

Traditional grape leaves stuffed with a flavorful rice mixture.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 1 jar grape leaves, rinsed
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

In a large bowl, mix rice, onion, pine nuts, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Place a spoonful of the mixture onto each grape leaf and roll it tightly. Place the rolled grape leaves in a pot, seam side down, and cover with water. Simmer on low heat for 40-45 minutes or until the rice is fully cooked. Serve warm or cold.

Chicken Rice

A comforting dish of tender chicken served over fragrant rice.

Read More

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 chicken thighs, skin on
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then sear on both sides until golden brown. Remove the chicken and set aside. In the same pot, add onions and garlic, cooking until softened. Stir in cumin and turmeric, then add rice and cook for a few minutes. Pour in the chicken broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Place the chicken thighs on top of the rice, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the chicken is tender. Serve hot.
